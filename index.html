FAQ

Incentia Consortium is a local chapter under department of CSE, SVCE aimed to enhance your technical and soft skills, with the motto - "Of the students, from the students, to the students."
All our events are aimed on enhancing the skill you most need in your engineering life. All of it is based on practicality which is necessarily required as an add-on to your theoretical knowledge. Above all, you get a platform to network.
All the events are held in SVCE campus.
As a student of SVCE, you are always a member of the consortium and welcome to attend the events held. Although, entries might be limited to the events.
